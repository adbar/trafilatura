Former WH press secretary Dee Dee Myers exits Warner Bros. role

By Ian Mohr

  • View author archive
  • email the author
  • follow on twitter
  • Get author RSS feed

February 24, 2020 | 9:25pm

Enlarge Image
Dee Dee Myers Getty Images

Dee Dee Myers a White House press secretary for Bill Clinton is exiting her role as communications head at studio Warner Bros. after five years.

Myers, who was EVP of worldwide corporate communications and public affairs, told Variety: Im going to take some time to figure out whats next. I dont have any set ideas.

But she added, At the same time, media has been a part of what Ive done for a long time. So I expect it will be connected in some way to what I do next. She also previously worked at power strategy firm Glover Park Group.

Her last day is April 1, and Warners has not named a replacement.
