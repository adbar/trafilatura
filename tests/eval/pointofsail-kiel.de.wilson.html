Ben Wilson Surf

So manch einer der eingefleischten Waverider kennt sie schon, die kompromisslos auf Waveriding und down-the-line Surf ausgelegt Boards und Kites von BWS.

Gerade ist die neue Palette Demokites und Boards eingetroffen. Hier etwas O-Ton: "BWSurf is the only surf specific kite company in the world. As surfers, our passion for waveriding drives us to design and manufacture purpose built equipment. We are 100% committed to creating the highest quality and performance products on the market and always strive to help you develop skills. Years of experience in some of the best (and worst) breaks all over the world allows us to understand what it takes to get you on the water and what you’ll need to get the most out of sessions. No matter where your travels take you, BWSurf will have you covered with the very best quality and value equipment. Our search for perfection never ends." - Ben Wilson -
